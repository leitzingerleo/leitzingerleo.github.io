I am a sixth-year PhD candidate in Economics at Goethe University Frankfurt. My research interests are in empirical corporate finance, law and economics, and innovation. My research centers on the mechanisms through which financially constrained firms alleviate financing frictions and expand access to capital. In 2024, I visited the Finance Department at Universitat Pompeu Fabra. I will be on the Finance Job Market in 2025.
